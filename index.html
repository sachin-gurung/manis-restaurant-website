Non-vegetarian Thali

A thali is a platter that is made up of several different dishes to create a perfectly balanced meal.

Momo

These are Nepali momos. They are type of steamed dumplings filled with some kind of meat or vegetables. It's the most famous delicacy of Nepal.

Paneer Curry

Paneer curry is a soupy dish made by braising paneer in a coconut milk soup base, flavoured with an aromatic curry spice paste, tamarind juices and seasonings

Nepal

Nepal is a country lying in the lap of the Himalayas, the world's highest mountain ranges. Nepalese food primarily consist of rice, lentils, vegetables and meat curries.

Visit Nepal

MasterChef Maniraj Gurung came from humble beginnings. Being born and raised in a village in Nepal, he spent most of his time helping his dad and mom at home. At the age of 18, he joined the British Army and worked as a Chef. He was constantly cooking up new savory dishes from United Kingdom as well as his homeland of Nepal on special occasions in order to feed the British troops. He soon deveoped a love for the different techniques used to cook many kinds of curries and the spices used in marinating the meat. He was inspired by how food can tell a story and bring people together on a table. He is retired now but he still enjoys cooking and experimenting new savory dishes at home.
